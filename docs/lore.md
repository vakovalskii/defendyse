# Lore — Defendyse

## Setting
Deep space. A lone planetary core — the last energy source of a dying civilization — drifts in the void. An endless swarm approaches from the dark: mindless, relentless, hungry for energy.

## The Core
The planet's exposed core pulses with raw energy. It can defend itself — barely — firing desperate energy bolts at nearby threats. But it cannot survive alone.

## The Swarm
Three castes of the swarm:

### Drones
Expendable organic drones. No intelligence, no strategy. They fly straight at the core and detonate on impact. Individually weak (1 HP), but overwhelming in numbers.

### Fighters
Enhanced organisms with ranged plasma glands. Faster than drones, tougher (4 HP), and capable of shooting energy projectiles at the core from a distance. They don't need to reach the core to damage it.

### Tanks
Massive armored bioforms. Slow (40 px/s) but incredibly tough (150 HP). When they reach the core, the impact is devastating (50 damage). They are the siege engines of the swarm.

## AA Towers (Defense Grid)
The player commands automated defense turrets placed in the void around the core:

- **Gatling**: Rapid-fire ballistic system. Shreds drones.
- **Cannon**: Heavy ordinance launcher. Punches through tank armor.
- **Laser**: High-energy beam weapon. Fast but short range.

## The War
Each wave is stronger than the last. The swarm adapts (future: LLM-driven evolution). The only question is: how long can the core survive?
