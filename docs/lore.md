# Lore — Defendyse

## Setting
Deep space. A lone planetary core — the last energy source of a dying civilization — drifts in the void. An endless swarm of organic creatures approaches from the dark: mindless, relentless, hungry for energy. The contrast is stark: cold, precise techno machines defend against writhing biological horrors.

## The Core
The planet's exposed core pulses with raw energy. It can defend itself — barely — firing desperate energy bolts at nearby threats. But it cannot survive alone. Its power radiates outward, fueling the defense grid — towers near the core operate at full strength (100%), while those at the far edge weaken to 30%.

## The Swarm
Three castes of organic creatures:

### Drones (Scarab Bugs)
Expendable organic scarab beetles. No intelligence, no strategy. They skitter straight at the core and detonate on impact. Individually weak (1 HP), but overwhelming in numbers. Their chitinous bodies gleam in the starlight.

### Fighters (Squid/Octopus)
Enhanced squid-like organisms with ranged plasma glands. Faster than drones, tougher (4 HP), and capable of shooting energy projectiles at the core from a distance. Their tentacles trail behind them as they glide through the void. They don't need to reach the core to damage it.

### Tanks (Crabs)
Massive armored crab bioforms. Slow (40 px/s) but incredibly tough (150 HP). When they reach the core, the crushing impact is devastating (50 damage). They are the siege engines of the swarm, their carapaces nearly impervious to light weapons.

## Defense Grid (Techno Machines)
The player commands automated techno defense towers placed in the void around the core. Each is a distinct machine with sharp geometric forms and neon glow:

- **Gatling**: Rapid-fire ballistic system. Green square housing spinning barrels. Shreds scarab drones.
- **Cannon**: Heavy ordinance launcher. Orange diamond frame containing a massive barrel. Punches through crab armor.
- **Laser**: High-energy beam weapon. Magenta triangle focusing crystal arrays. Fast but short range.
- **Hive**: Drone station. Hexagonal techno hangar that launches autonomous hunter drones. The drones seek and destroy enemies independently, growing stronger as the station is upgraded (+1 drone every 3 levels).

## Spirit Links
Towers can be connected through energy conduits called Spirit Links ($100 each). Linked towers resonate, boosting damage (+20%) and fire rate (+15%). When three towers form a Spirit Triangle, the energy between them creates a slow field (35% reduction) that saps the speed of organic creatures passing through.

## The War
Each wave is stronger than the last — enemies gain +8% HP per wave, scaling endlessly into the hundreds and thousands. The swarm adapts (future: LLM-driven evolution). The only question is: how long can the core survive?

## The Void
The battlefield is set against a living cosmos: three layers of parallax starfields drift past, nebulae pulse with color, and particle effects trail from every projectile and explosion. Floating damage numbers and screen shake punctuate each kill. The neon glow of the defense grid stands in stark contrast to the organic shapes of the swarm.
